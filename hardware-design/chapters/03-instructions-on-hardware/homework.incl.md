# Instructions on Hardware

In class we covered several strategies for instruction-level parallelism in the CPU:

- Out of Order Execution (OoO)
- Pipelining
- Simultaneous Multithreading (SMT) (aka Hyperthreading)
- Single Instruction, Multiple Data (SIMD)
- Superscalar Execution
- Very Long Instruction Word (VLIW)

Pick one. Make a piece of art about it. Medium of your choice (paint, crayon, poetry, collage, dance, tiktok, etc, etc). 

Include an artists's statement. 

Please spend at least ~3 hours on this assignment.
