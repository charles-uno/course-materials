Starting from the right, we add 1 and 1 to get 0 with a 1 to carry. Then, we add the carried digit to 1 and 0 to get 0 with another 1 to carry. This last carry gives us the leading 1.

We need three bits to represent the sum (with no overflow error). We’ll refer to the carry digits as carry_one (rightmost) and carry_two (leftmost, would be overflow if we were limited to two bits).
